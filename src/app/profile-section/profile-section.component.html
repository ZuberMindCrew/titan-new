Meet Carrie Moore, CEO/Founder

Carrie Moore is the CEO and Founder of Titan Edge Advisory, a trusted partner to fast-moving companies and leadership teams since 2016. Recognized by the Forbes Council for her contributions to business growth and leadership excellence, Carrie advises ambitious founders, executives, and boards on how to drive expansion, navigate organizational change, and unlock exponential results.

With over 25 years of experience as a strategist and capitalist, Carrie is widely regarded as the architect behind transformational growth initiatives that integrate system design, capital planning, and execution under pressure. She has been a first-call advisor during moments that define organizations, including financial crises, operational restructures, and complex change events across the energy, financial, and public sectors.

Carrie and her team of facilitators, coaches, consultants and staff quickly become trusted advisors to take your organizational performance to the next level.

Titan Edge Advisory's work and insights have been featured on stages and platforms including TEDx, Forbes, WSJ, Financial Times, ABC, NBC, AWS, Google for Startups, Facebook HQ, 100 Women in Finance, and more.
